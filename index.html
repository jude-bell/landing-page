About Features

Private Conversations. Self-Custody. Seamless.

Morse is the decentralized messenger built into a self-custodial Stellar wallet. You own your keys. You control your data. Connect and transact — privately.

No spam. Just launch updates and key announcements.

Core Principles

We built Morse around privacy, decentralization, and seamless usability.

End-to-End Encryption

Messages are encrypted locally. Only you and the intended recipient can read them.

Decentralized Storage

Content can be stored on decentralized nodes you choose — no central server in control.

On-Device Key Management

Your private keys never leave your device — identity is fully local.

Integrated Wallet

Send and receive Stellar (XLM) seamlessly in the same app — messaging + payments in one.

About Morse

Morse is developed by Morse Wallet, Inc. Our mission is to empower people with tools for private, sovereign communication and financial interactions. We believe messaging and payments should be integrated yet secure — no centralized gatekeepers, no surveillance.
